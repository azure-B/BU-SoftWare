Welcome to Baekseok.

Your definitive guide to navigating the first weeks of university life. From academic registration to finding your way around campus, begin your journey here.

Registration & Login Guide

01

Accessing the Student Hub

Navigate to the main Baekseok portal. Ensure you have your temporary credentials provided in your admission letter. These credentials expire 30 days after issuance.

02

Course Selection

Log into the SIS (Student Information System). Review the prerequisite requirements before confirming your schedule. Consult with your academic advisor if necessary.

03

Finalizing Registration

Submit your chosen courses for approval. You will receive an email confirmation once your faculty registrar has processed your selection.

Campus Life Tips

local_library

Library Access

Your student ID grants 24/7 access to the main academic library. Reserve study rooms via the campus app.

restaurant

Dining Halls

Three main dining facilities are available. Meal plans are loaded onto your student card automatically.

directions_bus

Shuttle Routes

Inter-campus shuttles run every 15 minutes during peak hours. Track them live on the student portal.

health_and_safety

Health Center

Located in Building B. Walk-ins are accepted for minor issues; appointments required for specialist consultations.
